Capabilities

Govern AI usage across your organization

From single sign-on to predictive alerting — every tool your marketing operation needs to stay in control.

🔐

Unified Single Sign-On

Store team credentials per service and inject them into login forms with one click. Stop sharing passwords in chat.

⚡

Automatic Credit Tracking

Every time a team member opens an AI dashboard, the extension silently records the remaining balance to your private database.

📊

Burn-Rate Analytics

Visualize 30-day usage trends, calculate the 7-day rolling consumption rate, and forecast the exact date credits will run out.

🌐

Plug-In Any Service

Add any AI platform by URL pattern. Sign-on works on Freepik out of the box, plus anything else you wire up.

Multiple Accounts per Service

Privacy by Design

Backend binds to 127.0.0.1 only. No cloud sync, no analytics, no telemetry. Your credentials and usage data never leave the machine.

Getting started

Operational in four steps

Deploy on your workstation in minutes — no infrastructure, no accounts, no subscriptions.

01

Install

Run the FastAPI backend locally. SQLite is included — no external database required.

02

Connect

Load the Chrome extension via Developer Mode. It auto-discovers the local backend on port 8765.

03

Configure

Open the admin console to register sites and add team credentials. Freepik is pre-seeded.

04

Operate

Sign on with one click, monitor consumption from the dashboard, and act before quotas run out.
